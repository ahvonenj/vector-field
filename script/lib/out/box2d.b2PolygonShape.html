Class: b2PolygonShape

b2PolygonShape

new b2PolygonShape()

A convex polygon. It is assumed that the interior of the polygon is to the left of each edge. Polygons have a maximum number of vertices equal to box2d.b2_maxPolygonVertices. In most cases you should not need many vertices for a convex polygon.
